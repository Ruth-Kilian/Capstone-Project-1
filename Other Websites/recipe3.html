Peanut Butter Biscuits

These are my mother's favourite biscuits.



Ingredients:

Prep time: 10 minutes
Baking time: 10-15 minutes
Serves: 30
  • 350ml flour
  • 2ml bicarb
  • 100g marg
  • 75g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2ml baking powder
  • Pinch salt
  • 30g peanut butter
  • 75g brown sugar

Method

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and bicarb together
  2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well
  3. Press into shapes and bake at 150˚C for 8 – 10mins
