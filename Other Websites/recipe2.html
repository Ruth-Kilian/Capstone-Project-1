Soetkoekies

These are my father's favourite cookies.



Ingredients:

Prep time: 10 minutes
Baking time: 10-15 minutes
Serves: 30
  • 500g self raising flour
  • 250g margarine
  • 500ml sugar
  • 3 eggs

Method

  1. Rub marg and sugar together.
  2. Add flour and mix well, now add the eggs
  3. Roll out and cut out
  4. Bake at 160ËšC until golden brown
