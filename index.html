About

From a very young age it was installed in my siblings and I that one day we would leave the house and then we would be responsible for ourselves. My mother involved us in cooking and cleaning ever since I can remember. I can recall standing on the kitchen chair just so that I could reach the countertop to mix the bowl or to wash the dishes.

Chef Gusteau from Ratatouille said, "Anyone can cook". And it is true. With determination and a little bit of patience, you can go from burning water to cooking gourmet courses. If you have the basics: olive oil, garlic, and onion; you can create any dish you want. AND if you follow the recipe, you cannot go wrong. (Cooking recipes don't have to be followed 100% unless you're a beginner).

Baking, on the other hand, is a type of science, if not followed down to every tiny detail, your item could flop. I have had countless baked items flop, especially cakes. My favourite confectionery to bake is biscuits. There are 5 biscuits that I make for my family. I have tried other biscuit recipes, but I always come back to theses 5.

"In the past, I have made no secret of my disdain for Chef Gusteau's famous motto, "Anyone can cook". But I realize, only now do I truly understand what he meant. Not everyone can become a great artist; but a great artist can come from anywhere."

- Anton Ego
